FoodShares is a winning project from the HackPNW Summer hackathon, designed to combat food insecurity. It connects surplus food from local businesses and individuals to those in need through a user-friendly platform. Inspired by Trader Joe's Neighborhood Shares program, FoodShares allows users to list excess food for donation and helps recipients locate nearby food banks or drop-off sites.
