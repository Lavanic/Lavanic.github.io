The Attention Button is a physical push button designed to combat digital distractions by triggering bright pink lights in my room, providing an immediate visual cue that someone is trying to get my attention. The button was modeled and 3D printed, integrating with Arduino MKR 1010, WiFiNINA, and IFTTT to deliver this functionality.
